NAME
       pop3d - manual page for pop3d (GNU Mailutils)

SYNOPSIS
       pop3d [OPTION...]

DESCRIPTION
       GNU pop3d -- the POP3 daemon

REPORTING BUGS
       Report bugs to <bug-mailutils@gnu.org>.

SEE ALSO
       The full documentation for pop3d is maintained as a Texinfo manual.  If
       the info and pop3d programs are properly installed at your site, the
       command

              info pop3d

       should give you access to the complete manual.
