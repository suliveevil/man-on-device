NAME
       imap4d - manual page for imap4d (GNU Mailutils)

SYNOPSIS
       imap4d [OPTION...]

DESCRIPTION
       GNU imap4d -- the IMAP4D daemon

REPORTING BUGS
       Report bugs to <bug-mailutils@gnu.org>.

SEE ALSO
       The full documentation for imap4d is maintained as a Texinfo manual.
       If the info and imap4d programs are properly installed at your site,
       the command

              info imap4d

       should give you access to the complete manual.
